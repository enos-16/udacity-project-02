German Shepherd

During World War I the German Shepherd served as a Red Cross dog, messenger, rescuer, guard, supply carrier, and sentry.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Lifespan: 9–13 years
  • Size: 22–26 inches
  • Habitat: Family Home

German Shepherds are well famous for their protectiveness over family members as well as their ability to learn new commands relatively quickly.
